## 📱 User Journey (Simplified)

```
1. User opens app
   ↓
2. Navigates to "Nearby" tab
   ↓
3. Sees nearby places with photos
   ↓
4. Filters by category or adjusts radius
   ↓
5. Taps place for quick action OR "View" for details
   ↓
6. Views comprehensive place information
   ↓
7. Takes action (call, email, navigate, share)
   ↓
8. Done - Contact established or directions started
```
